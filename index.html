About NSHMB

The Nasarawa State Hospitals Management Board (NSHMB) oversees the administration and management of public secondary healthcare facilities across the state. Our mission is to ensure efficient hospital operations, professional workforce management, and improved healthcare delivery systems.

Our Services

Hospital Administration

Supervision and coordination of public hospitals across Nasarawa State.

Healthcare Workforce Management

Recruitment, training, and management of healthcare professionals.

Digital Health Records

Implementation of modern digital systems for patient and hospital data management.
